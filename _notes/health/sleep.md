In this notes I try to answer the following questions:
- Which factors determine the duration of my natural sleep (natural meaning without clock alarm)?
- Which factors determine how well-rested I feel after a sleep?

I try to answer these questions by reading scientific literature and observing my sleep. 
I am not organized enough to create a systematic dataset on my sleep, so I will just go for "random" measures, and hope that at a certain point I will have enough coherent data to draw some significant statement.
I will record data with the help of my Fitbit Charge 5.
The variables that I will record depend on what I will learn from the literature.
